Privacy Policy

How is my data collected?

All data is exclusively collected on your current local device.

What are my rights?

Any information (personal or medical) is protected by HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act). No information will be gathered unless you are explicitly notified prior to providing any self-identifying or medical information.

How is my information protected?

We have taken reasonable measures to prevent any malicious attacks which may seek your personal data. In addition, none of the information you provide will be transmitted over the Internet or saved to your local device. However, all information provided for this website is voluntary and done at one's own risk.

When and why is my data being collected?

Any time that information is required (e.g. completing a form), you will be notified through a Privacy Disclosure before providing any information. In addition, we will inform you of the purpose of the information gathered with Terms and Conditions which you must agree to prior to providing any personal, medical, or potentially self-identifying information.
