Hospice Eligibility

Advocate Hospice strives to provide excellent hospice and palliative care to eligible patients who benefit tremendously from hospice benefits. As such, we follow Medicare's Hospice Eligibility Guidelines to ensure that we are provide the best outcome for patients, whether they come into out service or not.
