Privacy Policy

How is my data collected?

No data that you provide will be saved permanently. All data is exclusively collected on your current local device and never sent to an external server.

What are my rights?

Any information (personal or medical) is protected by HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act). Furthermore, no information will be gathered unless you are explicitly notified with a Privacy Disclosure prior to providing any self-identifying or medical information.

How is my information protected?

None of the information you provide will be transmitted over the Internet or saved to your local device. In addition, we have taken measures to protect any data that you provide, however, all information provided for this website is voluntary and done at one's own risk.

When and why is my data being collected?

Any time that information is required (e.g. completing a form), you will be notified through a Privacy Disclosure before providing any information. In addition, we will inform you of the purpose of the information gathered with Terms and Conditions which you must agree to prior to providing any personal, medical, or potentially self-identifying information.
